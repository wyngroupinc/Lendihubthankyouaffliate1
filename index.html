Before You Decide

Questions you probably have

We'd rather answer them here than hide them in fine print.

Will this hurt my credit score?

We don't pull your credit at all. Not a hard pull, not a soft pull — nothing. We match you based on the basic info you provide. If you formally apply with a specific lender later, that's between you and them.

What's the catch? How is this free?

Lenders pay us a referral fee when they fund a loan. We only make money when you get funded with terms you chose. If you walk away, we earn zero. Our entire model depends on finding you a deal good enough to accept.

Am I committing to anything?

No. 100% non-binding. See what's available, and if nothing fits — you're done. No follow-up obligations, no contracts, no hidden terms. Close the tab and never think about it again.

Will I get bombarded with calls?

Only lenders with real, matched offers reach out. We don't sell your info to random companies. You hear from actual lending partners with something concrete — not telemarketers reading a script.

My credit isn't perfect. Can I still check?

Absolutely. Many of our partners specialize in business owners with imperfect credit. They look at revenue, time in business, and industry — not just a number. You might be surprised.

How is this different from my bank?

Your bank gives you one option — theirs. We put 75+ lenders in front of you at once. More competition means better rates and terms than any single institution would offer on its own.

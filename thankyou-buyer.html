How to get the best offer possible

A little prep goes a long way. Do these 3 things before your call.

1
Answer your phone — even unknown numbers

Your funding specialist will call from a number you don't recognize. If you miss it, check your voicemail — they'll leave a message with a callback number.

2
Have your bank statements handy

3–6 months of business bank statements will speed up the process significantly. Having them ready shows lenders you're serious and can get you faster approval.

3
Know your numbers (roughly)

Approximate monthly revenue, time in business, and how much you're looking for. You don't need exact figures — ballpark is fine. Your specialist will help with the rest.
